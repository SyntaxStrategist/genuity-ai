Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2, 2025

TL;DR: Your data never leaves your device. We can't see it, access it, or share it. Ever.

1. Introduction

Genuity AI ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mental health tracking application.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Mood Entries

You provide:

  • Mood ratings (1-5 scale)
  • Text descriptions of your feelings
  • Activities you've engaged in
  • Timestamps of entries

2.2 HealthKit Data (Optional)

With your explicit permission, we may access:

  • Sleep data (hours and quality)
  • Exercise minutes (from Apple Health)
  • Step count

Important: This data is only used locally on your device for pattern analysis. It is never transmitted or stored externally.

2.3 Data We DO NOT Collect

  • ❌ No personal identification information
  • ❌ No location data
  • ❌ No contact information
  • ❌ No device identifiers
  • ❌ No usage analytics
  • ❌ No crash reports sent to servers

3. How We Store Your Data

100% Local Storage
All your data is stored exclusively on your iPhone or iPad using Apple's secure iOS Keychain. This means your mood entries are encrypted using your device passcode and never leave your device.

3.1 Encryption

Your mood data is encrypted using iOS Keychain, which is protected by:

  • Your device passcode
  • Face ID or Touch ID (if enabled)
  • Apple's hardware-level security

3.2 No Cloud Sync

Genuity AI does not sync your data to iCloud, our servers, or any third-party service. Your data exists only on the device where you created it.

4. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively on your device to:

  • Detect patterns in your mood over time
  • Generate predictions about future mood states
  • Provide personalized prevention suggestions
  • Show effectiveness reports

All processing happens locally on your device. We never see, access, or analyze your data.

5. AI Processing

5.1 Local AI (Default)

By default, all AI processing (pattern detection, predictions, insights) happens entirely on your device using local algorithms. No data is sent anywhere.

5.2 Cloud AI (Optional)

If you choose to enable Cloud AI mode for enhanced conversational responses:

  • Only your chat messages are sent to OpenAI's API
  • Your mood data, patterns, and predictions remain local
  • Messages are sent over encrypted HTTPS
  • OpenAI's privacy policy applies to this data
  • You can disable this at any time in Settings

6. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or disclose your data to anyone. Period.

Because your data never leaves your device, we have no ability to share it even if we wanted to.

7. Your Rights

7.1 Access Your Data

You can view all your data within the app at any time in the History tab.

7.2 Export Your Data

Settings → Data → Export Data provides a JSON file of all your mood entries that you can save or share as you choose.

7.3 Delete Your Data

Settings → Data → Delete All Data permanently removes all your information from the app. This action cannot be undone.

8. Children's Privacy

Genuity AI is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you are under 13, please do not use this app.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app. The "Last Updated" date at the top indicates when changes were made.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at:
hello@genuityai.com

11. Legal Compliance

Genuity AI complies with applicable data protection laws, including GDPR (for EU users) and CCPA (for California users).

Bottom Line: Your mental health data is sensitive. We treat it with the highest level of security by keeping it exclusively on your device. We can't access it, we can't share it, and we never will.
